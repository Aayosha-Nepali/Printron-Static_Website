# Homepage

We used a Shopify template as a reference for our homepage, specifically the hero section. We liked how they set up the big hero section at the top and used it as a guide to make our own homepage look nice and easy to use.

# Products

For Products page, we have list the products by implementing one of the product card example from ecommerce product card. Each card has:

  • Product Image: The first thing you see.
  • Product Title: The name of the item.
  • Rating: Stars from Font Awesome.
  • Pricing: How much it costs.
  • Add to Cart Button: A button to put the item in your cart.

For Rating Icons, we have use font-awesome icon.

# Blogs

For our blogs page, we looked at the blog layout design from W3Schools. Our page has:

  • Blog List: A list of all our blog posts.
  • Popular Blogs: A section for popular posts.
  • Follow Us Card: A card to help you follow us on social media.

We used CSS Flexbox and CSS Float Layout to make everything look nice and organized.

# Contact Form

For our contact form, we used HTML to create the form and JavaScript for checking the information before submission. When you submit the form, an alert box will pop up to show a message confirming that your form was sent.
